About Me

I am a full stack software developer with a background in the arts and the service industry. In the fall of 2019, I decided to pursue a new career in the tech industry, and enrolled in Momentum Learning's Immersive Full Stack Development Program, an intensive, 12 week coding bootcamp in Durham, NC. I have developed applications, both individually and collaboratively, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Django, and utilizing design principles such as Object-Oriented Programming. I love collaboration, and have experience with the Agile Development Methodology.


Outside of coding, my passions lie in the arts. In college I studied film and have experience as an independent filmmaker, and currently serve as co-host of the weekly horror film review podcast, Pod People. I also love video games and moonlight as an indie game dev for Light Arc Studio, where I work as the lead writer and script director. When I'm not working, I enjoy reading, cooking, playing video games, spending time with friends and my cat, and writing short horror stories.
